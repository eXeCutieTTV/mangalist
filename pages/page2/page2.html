On using the site
  • To change the owned-state of an entry, simply press the green/red label seen bellow said entry, and watch it change color to confirm the change. If the entry is displayed as green, then I own the entry, if it is red, i do not.
  • To update the list, simply press the "Update list" button, at the top of the page. This will create a new savefile, and will require you to refresh the page to see if it has taken effect.
  • To filter the order of the series' displayal, use the "Filter" dropdownmenu, at the top of the page. Press an again to alternate between ascending/descending.
  • To filter which entries are visible for a given entry, simply press the given entry's header. Press it again to alternate between filters - Unowned, Owned, All. Hold the header for 1 second to manualy pick an option.
